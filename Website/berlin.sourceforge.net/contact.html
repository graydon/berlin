Contact

The Fresco project is run by volunteers from around the globe.

Check the people page to find out who we are and how to contact each of us. For information on how to chat with us look at our IRC page. For normal questions the Mailing lists are the best place to go. We have a special mailing list for announcements. Fresco-announce is a very low traffic list and ideal if you want to keep an eye on what we are doing from afar.

The code powering this site was written by Alexander Johannessen, using XML and XSLT. The sources are available under the GNU GPL from CVS. The actual data is available under the GNU FPL from the same place. To report problems with this site, please contact our webmaster.
